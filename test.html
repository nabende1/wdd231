Local Art Hub

Site Name

Local Art Hub

This name represents a dedicated platform for showcasing local artists and promoting art events in the community. It emphasizes the connection between art and the local culture.

Optional domain availability: localarthub.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to serve as a comprehensive resource for local art enthusiasts by providing information on featured artists, upcoming art events, and art tips. Users can discover new artists, find events to attend, and learn more about art-related topics.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming art events are happening in my area?
  • How can I find local artists and their work?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #4A90E2 (used for headings and buttons)

Secondary Color: #F5A623 (used for accents and highlights)

Typography

Heading Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif (used for all headings)

Body Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif (used for all body text)

Wireframe Description

Mobile View

Header with logo and hamburger menu, full-width hero with call-to-action, stacked sections (Featured Artists, Upcoming Events, Art Tips), and footer with contact info and social icons.

Desktop View

Header with logo and horizontal navigation, three-column layout for content (Featured Artists, Upcoming Events, Art Tips), and a footer split into contact info, additional links, and social icons.
